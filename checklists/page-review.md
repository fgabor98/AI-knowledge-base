# Page Review Checklist

Use this checklist before changing a page from `status: draft` to `status: reviewed`.

- The topic has a clear scope.
- The page explains what problem the topic solves.
- Core concepts are accurate.
- Examples are concrete and runnable or clearly marked as illustrative.
- Commands include expected context and privileges where relevant.
- Common mistakes are specific and useful.
- Debugging steps are actionable.
- Embedded Linux implications are covered where relevant.
- References point to primary or reputable sources.
- AI-generated claims have been manually checked.
- Related topics link to existing or planned pages.
- YAML frontmatter is updated after review.
